Welcome to Kolkata-The City Of Joy

Kolkata, a city that celebrates culture, creativity, and charm. From heritage to modern marvels, the city offers something for every traveler.

Biswa Bangla Gate is one of Kolkata’s most iconic modern landmarks, located in the heart of New Town. Designed in the shape of a grand arch, it features a stunning glass viewing gallery suspended high above the ground. This architectural marvel represents Bengal’s rich heritage blended with futuristic vision. From the top, visitors can enjoy a breathtaking 360-degree view of the cityscape. At night, the gate comes alive with beautiful lighting, making it a popular spot for photography and leisure walks. The gallery also houses a fine-dining restaurant, offering a unique sky-high culinary experience. As a proud gateway to New Town, it welcomes everyone with elegance and charm.

Eco Park, located in New Town, Kolkata, is one of the largest urban parks in India. It offers a perfect blend of nature, recreation, and entertainment for visitors of all ages.Visitors can enjoy activities like boating, cycling, and nature walks in its lush green surroundings. One of the major attractions is the replica of the Seven Wonders of the World, which draws a lot of tourists. The park also hosts musical fountains, open-air events, and food courts for a complete family outing. With its peaceful environment and wide range of attractions, Eco Park is a favorite weekend getaway for both locals and tourists.

Nazrul Tirtha is a cultural and educational center in New Town, Kolkata, dedicated to the legendary Bengali poet Kazi Nazrul Islam. The building’s modern architectural design reflects creativity and artistic spirit. It hosts regular film screenings, exhibitions, seminars, and cultural programs celebrating Nazrul’s contributions. The center also has a museum that showcases his life, works, and legacy. Nazrul Tirtha stands as a vibrant hub for art, literature, and culture in the city.

Mother’s Wax Museum, located in New Town, Kolkata, was established in 2014 and named in honor of Mother Teresa. It features over 50 life-size wax statues of famous personalities from various fields like cinema, sports, politics, music, and literature. Visitors can see figures like Mahatma Gandhi, Rabindranath Tagore, Shah Rukh Khan, Lionel Messi, and many more. The museum offers a fun and interactive experience for people of all ages. Situated right opposite Eco Park, it is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the city.

Harinalaya Mini Zoo, located in New Town, Kolkata, spans 12.5 acres and was established in 2017. Initially a deer park, it has expanded to house a variety of animals, including spotted and barking deer, zebras, giraffes, and a hippopotamus. The zoo features naturalistic enclosures designed to provide an immersive experience for visitors. Open daily from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, except Thursdays, it offers an educational and recreational outing for families and wildlife enthusiasts. Harinalaya stands as a serene destination for those seeking a closer connection with nature within the city.
